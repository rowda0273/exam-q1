About Us

The Uganda Wildlife Conservation Society (UWCS) is dedicated to protecting Uganda's wildlife and ecosystems. Our mission is to ensure a sustainable environment for future generations by engaging local communities, educating the public, and supporting scientific research.

We focus on both wildlife protection and habitat restoration efforts, working with various stakeholders to make a lasting impact on the biodiversity of Uganda.

Wildlife & Conservation Projects

Gorilla Protection Project

Our efforts focus on protecting endangered mountain gorillas in Uganda through anti-poaching, habitat restoration, and public education programs.
